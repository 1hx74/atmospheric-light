The enclosure consists of three parts. It was printed using **Standard PETG** filament with a **0.2 mm layer height**. A horizontal expansion (horizontal compensation) of **-0.07 mm** was applied during printing.

Unfortunately, assembling the project inside this enclosure turned out to be somewhat difficult due to limited experience in 3D modeling — certain design considerations for easy assembly were not taken into account from the beginning. However, assembly is still possible. The enclosure will be redesigned in the future to address these issues.

### Components used:
- Arduino Nano  
- Sliding encoder  
- 12×12 mm push button  
- 470 Ω resistor  
- 470 mF, 6.3 V capacitor  
- Quick-release DC power connector  
- DS1110-01-4 connector  

The assembly process can be seen in the attached photos.
